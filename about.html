Who We Are

Excellence in Integrated Security Solutions

First Solutions Limited (FSL) is a professionally managed company providing Integrated Security Services, Facility Management Solutions, Electronic Security Systems, and Office Stationery & Supply Services to banks, financial institutions, multinational companies, corporate offices, factories, and government organizations across Bangladesh.

The company is recognized for its professional standards, operational discipline, reliability, and commitment to client satisfaction. FSL combines experienced leadership, trained manpower, and modern security solutions to deliver dependable and cost-effective services tailored to institutional and corporate requirements.

The management team of First Solutions Limited comprises experienced security industry professionals with extensive expertise in large-scale security operations, compliance management, risk mitigation, and corporate governance. Their collective experience enables FSL to maintain international-standard practices and deliver quality-driven security solutions.

The company is led by Mr. Selim Chowdhury, a seasoned security industry leader with extensive experience in managing professional security operations. Under his leadership, FSL has developed a reputation for professionalism, disciplined operations, efficient service delivery, and strong client relationship management.
