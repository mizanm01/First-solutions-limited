Who We Are

First Solutions Limited (FSL) is Bangladesh's premier professionally managed security company, providing Integrated Security Services, Facility Management, Electronic Security Solutions, and Corporate Supply Solutions to leading banks, multinational corporations, and government organizations. Backed by a highly experienced management team comprising seasoned security professionals, FSL brings extensive expertise in large-scale security operations, operational excellence, and international-standard service delivery. Our leadership combines industry knowledge, disciplined management practices, and a commitment to innovation to provide reliable, customized security solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients.
